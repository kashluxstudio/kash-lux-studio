Effective date: July 11, 2026

1. Information we collect

When you submit a consultation or contact form, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, business name, selected service, estimated budget, project details, and any other information you choose to provide.

2. How information is used

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, evaluate project needs, prepare recommendations or quotes, provide services, communicate about projects, maintain business records, prevent misuse, and improve our services.

3. Forms and service providers

Website forms are processed through FormSubmit and delivered to Ka$h Lux Studio’s Outlook inbox. Payment links direct customers to PayPal, which processes payment and account information under its own privacy policy. This website is hosted through GitHub Pages. These providers may process technical or personal information according to their own terms and privacy practices.

4. Payment information

Ka$h Lux Studio does not collect or store complete card or bank details through this website. Payments are processed by PayPal. We may receive transaction details such as the payer’s name, email address, payment amount, date, and payment status.

5. Sharing information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers needed to operate the website, process payments, communicate with clients, deliver contracted work, comply with law, protect rights and safety, or complete a business transfer.

6. Data retention and security

Information is kept only as long as reasonably needed for inquiries, client services, records, legal obligations, and dispute prevention. Reasonable safeguards are used, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

7. Your choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of information held by Ka$h Lux Studio, subject to legal, financial, contractual, and recordkeeping requirements. You may also ask not to receive non-essential marketing communication.

8. Children’s privacy

This website and its business services are not directed to children under 13, and Ka$h Lux Studio does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Updates and contact

This policy may be updated when website practices, services, or legal requirements change. Questions or privacy requests can be sent to HHR1000@Outlook.com.

This general website policy is not legal advice. Additional disclosures or rights may apply depending on where Ka$h Lux Studio and its customers are located.
